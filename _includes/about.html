Venezia Ventures, Inc.

Venezia Ventures, Inc. is an entrepreneurial company. The primary focus is on complex Information Technology challenges in the Data Center Administration space. Additional energy goes into tangential enterprises.

Our expertise includes:

Fiber optic back-haul to data center facilities, Tier 1 Internet transit provider and IP address management, and Cisco network project management.



Power systems infrastructure including solar farms, diesel and natural gas generators, double conversion UPS systems, controllable PDUs, and site and rack level Automatic Transfer Switches.



Purpose-built, cost-effective rack mount server component selection, especially relating to tiered storage design using ZFS.



Software stack including OmniOS, Solaris, Mac, Linux, Windows, Java, Grails, Domino, Jekyll, and more.
